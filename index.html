This is the tea you were looking for.

Teayamo brings you the freshest and finest of Indian teas in directly from tea gardens and blenders of Darjeeling and Assam within shortest possible time from their production. Teayamo is derived from Spanish word “te amo” which means “I Love you”, and our love for teas made it irresistible for us the brand the world willlove for finest teas.


Why Teayamo?

Fresh tea is far tastier and enjoyable than regular tea you have beend served till now. Normally tea waits for about a month before being packaged and then few more months before it reaches you. Teayamo often reduces this source to home time to 15 days, and you get finest teas packaged in airtight foils or in nitrogen sealed pouches. Our tasters under the guidance of Mr. C P Sharma check every tea for its texture, aroma, palate, cup, taste and purity, and then rate them for you to choose the tea as per your preferences. Teayamo?

For someone who loves tea, Teayamo becomes an obvious natural choice.

Sanjay Goel, Founder- Teayamo

Legacy carried forward

Teayamo is founded by 1st generation entrepreneur, Sanjay Goel under the mentorship of Mr. C P Sharma, a veteran into tea industry, who has spent over 40 years handling full plantation at various teas gardens of Assam. Mr. Sharma has twice been unanimously elected as the Chairman of Tea Association of India (TAIAB), Assam branch, for driving innovation in tea plantation.
